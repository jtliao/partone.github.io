The Colour Theory Myth
COLOUR THEORY is a concept that was contrived in the 18th century by various European thinkers as a means of controlling the way we see the world around us.

Colour theory is an intentionally convoluted and enigmatic system that fabricates relationships between colours through systemic classification and hierarchy.
